## What is Castra?

Castra is a mod planet for Factorio (Space Age). Its defining mechanic is a "tech copying" enemy faction that researches technologies independently of the player — and does so aggressively. Castra is the sole source of **nickel batteries**, which are required for promethium science pack production, making it a necessary destination for late-game progression.

The original mod is currently unmaintained.

---

## Why This Fork?

The original Castra mod has a well-documented late-game balance problem: the enemy faction's research is uncapped and unrestricted, leading to situations where enemies possess artillery with effectively infinite range, nuclear weapons, and dense landmine fields that make both base defense and personal combat non-viable. Community feedback confirms this renders the mod unplayable in long saves.

This fork aims to preserve everything that makes Castra interesting — the tech-copying faction, the unique challenge, the nickel battery resource — while fixing the mechanics that make it unfair rather than difficult.

## Changes from Original

### v1 — Playable (current focus)

- **Artillery range cap**: Enemy artillery range research is capped at a reasonable maximum, preventing the base-destruction feedback loop that makes passive defense impossible.
- **Landmine rebalance**: Landmines no longer fully root the player on trigger. Reduced to a movement speed penalty, preventing the stun-into-nuke instakill scenario.
- **Enemy nuke restrictions**: Removed or restricted enemy access to nuclear weapons in personal/direct combat contexts.
- **Research rate tuning**: Enemy tech research rate reduced to a level that feels threatening but not hopeless.
- **Notification reduction**: Reduced or made optional the spam of enemy research completion notifications.

### Planned — v2 (Polish)

- Filter irrelevant technologies from enemy research pool (productivity techs, planet discovery, etc.)
- Achievement fix: enemy research should not trigger player achievements
- Forge building rebalance
- Combat Roboport usability improvements
- Uranium ore spawn rate review
- Bug fixes from original mod issue tracker
- Merge pending German localization PR

## Compatibility

- Factorio **2.0+** with **Space Age**
- Designed to be save-compatible with existing Planet Castra saves, provided prototype names are unchanged (see migration notes below)

### Upgrading from Planet Castra

If you are mid-playthrough on the original Castra mod, you should be able to swap in Castra Prime without losing save progress, as all prototype names are preserved. The enemy faction's already-researched technologies will remain researched, but future research will be subject to the new caps and rate limits.

> **Note:** Always back up your save before switching mods mid-playthrough.
